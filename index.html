ABOUT DJ MAJESTIK

DJ Majestik’s numerous accomplishments in radio and the music industry have earned him recognition among artists and music groups all over. Besides being one of the select DJs to be both an on-air personality and mix show DJ in radio, Majestik has also performed at events with top music artists in many parts of the world. He has made a number of television appearances with broadcast companies such as FOX network and Discovery Channel. He has also participated in nationwide tours and lifestyle campaigns, working with recognized brands that include Red Bull, Nike, and more.


Majestik continues to leave an impression on club goers, both locally and internationally. With style and skill that separates him from the rest, he has traveled to play elite clubs around the globe, touring cities that include Berlin, Manila, Montreal, and Shanghai.


DJ Majestik has also added satellite radio to his extensive resume, being a featured mixer on world-renowned artist, Pitbull’s ‘Globalization’ Channel on Sirius XM Radio. Majestik most recently returned to Southern California to join “The New Guys” Morning Show on 97.1 AMP Radio.
